About Us

As a fellowship of believers under the Lordship of Jesus Christ, we aspire to serve the spiritual needs of our community while staying rooted in Biblical doctrines. Our church is a sanctuary for those seeking spiritual nourishment, emotional support, and a sense of belonging. We hold fast to the inerrancy of the Scriptures as the ultimate guide for our lives. Through various ministries, including our Christian School and outreach programs, we extend Christ’s love beyond our walls. Our congregation is diverse, yet united in our desire to live according to God’s will.

Get to Know Us!

Core Beliefs

  • Foundation: Jesus Christ mends our broken relationship with God through His sacrifice.
  • Spirit: Empowers us to live faith-filled lives.
  • Community: We are a fellowship of believers, united in love and purpose.
  • Scripture: The Bible is our ultimate guide and authority.
  • Outreach: Committed to sharing the Gospel and serving the community.
